A History of Innovation
in Travel.

Our Story
01
2008
The Origin of Thayer
Founded by Chris Hemmeter with a conviction that travel technology was on the verge of a generational transformation — and that a sector-focused venture partner could help define it.
2008

The Origin of Thayer

Chris Hemmeter founded Thayer Ventures with a singular focus: backing the technology companies reshaping travel and mobility. At the time, few investors saw the sector's potential. Thayer was built to prove them wrong.

2012

First Thayer Fund

Chris Hemmeter teams up with Lee Pillsbury to launch the first Thayer Fund. Lee brought 19 years at Marriott — including an Executive Vice President role heading Lodging Strategy — giving the fund an unmatched operator perspective from day one.

2018

First Institutional Fund

Chris Hemmeter, Jeff Jackson, and Tyler Carrico launch Thayer's first Institutional Fund. Jeff's 14 years at American Airlines and Tyler's investment experience deepened the team's reach across aviation, distribution, and enterprise travel technology.

2022

Second Institutional Fund

The launch of Thayer's second Institutional Fund reflects a decade of compounding relationships, returns, and sector knowledge. The fund was raised against the backdrop of travel's post-pandemic recovery — a moment that validated every counter-cyclical bet the firm had made.

2022

First Derive Fund

Tyler Carrico and Mike Scott co-found Derive Ventures, expanding the Thayer platform with a dedicated early-stage fund. Mike's background in private equity at KSL Capital Partners added a new analytical lens to the platform's investment approach.

Investment Philosophy
02
01 — Conviction
Sector depth over breadth
We invest only in travel and hospitality technology. This concentration gives us pattern recognition, proprietary deal flow, and the ability to add genuine value beyond capital.
02 — Network
Distribution as a moat
Our portfolio companies benefit from direct introductions to our network of over 400 industry executives, channel partners, and co-investors across 60 countries.
03 — Partnership
Operators backing operators
Every partner at Thayer has worked inside the industry they fund. We have been in the founder's seat. That context shapes every board conversation, every strategic pivot, every hire we help make.
